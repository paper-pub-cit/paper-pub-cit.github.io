Research Paper Publication and Cross-city Citation Globe

Based on the concept of spatial scientometrics, we extract the geographical information contained in the institutions of literature authors to reveal the spatial distribution of scientific research output at the city level. From the perspective of the number of publications, we statistically analyze the scientific knowledge productivity of cities worldwide. Under the guidance of knowledge flow theory, we construct a scientific knowledge flow network between geographical spatial knowledge subjects at the city scale, linked by the citation relationships between literatures. From the perspective of citation influence, we statistically analyze the scientific knowledge influence of cities worldwide. By comprehensively examining the scientific knowledge productivity and influence of cities, and combining the visualization of city research output and cross-city citation relationships on geographical maps, we analyze the spatial agglomeration characteristics of scientific knowledge. According to certain standards, we identify highlands of scientific knowledge at the city level.
