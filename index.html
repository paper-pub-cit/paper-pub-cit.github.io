Research Paper Publication
& Cross-city Citation Globe

This research uses spatial scientometrics to analyze the geographical distribution of scientific research output and influence at the city level.
It constructs a scientific knowledge flow network based on citation relationships, visualizes city research output and cross-city citation relationships, and identifies highlands of scientific knowledge according to certain standards.
